Privacy Policy

Ekichō collects minimal personal data necessary to support account login and basic app functionality.

When you sign in with Apple, we collect a unique user identifier and a private relay email address (e.g. @privaterelay.appleid.com).

We also store your selected home station and city ID in a secure database using Firebase. This data is used only to personalize your app experience and is not shared with third parties.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are used.
